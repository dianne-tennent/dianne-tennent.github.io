My Learning Plan

10-Feb-2021

Long term goal and/or career pathway

Long term I would like to work as a software developer for educational software. I would like to be able to use digital tools to meet a range of learning needs, particularly in the area of literacy.

Human skills I’d like to develop while at EDA

I would really like to develop a strong worth ethic and good habits when it comes to managing time and productivity. I’d like to get further experience working harmoniously and productively within a team environment. I’d also like to develop my “big picture” thinking, rather than getting lost in too many details.

My strengths and limitations

I’m a very detail oriented person, good at problem solving with a passion for creativity. On the flipside of this, I can sometimes spend too much time on perfecting and refining things, and considering a variety of potential approaches. This can be perfectionistic at times and get in the way of producing a satisfactory outcome, and “not seeing the wood for the trees.”

How I will manage my workload and work productively and safely with other learners, facilitators and industry/community representatives.

Create a clear and detailed plan of what needs to be completed and by when. Trello could be a useful tool for this. I will manage myself to work productively and safely with other learners, facilitators and industry/community representatives by:

  • Being professional and courteous at all times
  • Communicating respectfully with others
  • Communicating clearly and in a timely manner regarding meetings and work deadlines
  • Sharing my knowledge with others
  • Being supportive of other learners

How and where I will seek help in a timely way

I will seek help by contacting my facilitator or other learners. I will do this in advance of project due dates and not at the last minute.

What I expect from the facilitation team

I would like the facilitation team to check in with me about my progress and give me feedback on the work that I’ve completed.

Scheduling information

I don’t have any other commitments, but it is important that I schedule in time for my own well-being and quality time with my partner, friends and family.
