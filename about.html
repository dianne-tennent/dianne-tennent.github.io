About Me

Haere mai. Ko Dianne toku ingoa. Ko Lower Hutt toku kainga.

I’m doing EDA because I think tech has a huge potential to support the learning needs of our young people and I want to be in a position where I can develop tools that support learning, particularly around literacy.

The thing I value most is inclusion.

Inclusion in all areas such as race, culture, gender, sexuality, but also just general inclusion with regard to respecting and taking into account a range of points of view, with the goal being an outcome that meets everyone’s needs. Another important value for me is forgiveness - of ones self and of others. We’re all human and it’s so important to give ourselves and others a break and create a supportive environment within which we can all flourish.
